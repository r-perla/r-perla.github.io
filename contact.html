Weekly Meeting

We host a weekly lab meeting (Tuesdays at 2 pm) to discuss ongoing projects as well as relevant methods, theories, and papers. We are more than happy to welcome guests! If you are interested in giving a presentation, asking questions, or joining our discussions, feel free to contact us. At the moment, the meeting is usually in a hybrid format (partly in person and partly online).

Join Us

We are looking for people at all levels:

  • Student assistants ("Hiwis," "studentische Hilfskräfte") or interns
  • Bachelor or Master students
  • Students who want to pursue a PhD or a medical doctorate (MD)
  • Postdocs

Please indicate in your email your interests, your experience, and your skills. We also support people applying for their own funding. Possible funding options include Marie Curie schemes or the new DFG program Walter Benjamin. Our research focuses on cognitive neuroscience but overlaps with many different research areas (e.g., game theory, evolutionary biology), so people from other backgrounds (e.g., physics, maths, computer science, biology) are also encouraged to apply.
